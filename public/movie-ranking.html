The Best Movies According to Uğur Can

My top 3 Turkish movies of all-time.

Yedinci Koğuştaki Mucize

Dramatic story of a disabled father and his daughter. Tissue is recommended!

Bizim İçin Şampiyon

Story of one of the best racehorses of Turkey. It shows the relation between the horse, its owner, and its jockey. Very deep!

Eşkıya

You cannot understand without watching it. Just watch it!
